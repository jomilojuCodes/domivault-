2.Sensitive identity documents

Special handling: Your NIN, CAC documents, and live selfie are collected solely to verify landlord identity and prevent fraud. These documents are:
  • Encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Accessible only to authorized Domivault verification staff
  • Never sold, rented, or shared with third-party advertisers
  • Never displayed publicly on any listing or profile
  • Used only for the verification process and not for any other purpose without your separate consent

3.How we use your information

  • To verify landlord identity before allowing them to list a property
  • To physically inspect and confirm listed properties
  • To connect tenants and landlords through messaging and enquiries
  • To personalize listings shown to you (e.g. by location or saved preferences)
  • To send service notifications (viewing reminders, messages, account alerts)
  • To improve the platform, investigate fraud, and maintain trust and safety

4.Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information in these cases:

  • Between users: When you message a landlord or tenant, your name and message content are shared with that user.
  • Inspection officers: Property details and landlord contact information are shared with our inspection team to schedule and conduct visits.
  • Service providers: We may use trusted third parties for hosting, analytics, or payment processing, bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by Nigerian law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5.Data storage & security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. While we take strong precautions, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6.Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide our services. Identity verification documents are retained only as long as necessary for verification and compliance purposes, after which they may be securely deleted upon request or account closure, subject to legal record-keeping requirements.

7.Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location services or notifications at any time via account settings
  • Object to certain uses of your data, where applicable under Nigerian data protection law (NDPR)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@domivault.ng.

8.Cookies & tracking

Domivault may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9.Children's privacy

Domivault is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will take steps to delete that information.

10.Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of material changes through the platform or via email.

11.Contact us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@domivault.ng.

Operating in Nigeria: Domivault aims to handle personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and related regulations.
