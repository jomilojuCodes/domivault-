The Problem

Fraudsters pose as agents and disappear with buyers' money.

🏚️ Fake listings

Many online properties don't exist or are heavily misrepresented.

How Domivault Works

Verification before visibility.

Every listing on Domivault goes through a rigorous inspection process before a single buyer ever sees it.

1
Agent onboarding

Agents verify their identity

Realtors submit their NIN, CAC registration, and professional credentials. We verify every agent before they can list a single property.

2
Listing submission

Agent submits a property

Verified agents upload property details, photos, price, and location. The listing enters our inspection queue immediately.

3
Our core differentiator

Our team physically visits the property

A Domivault inspection officer visits every property in person — confirming it exists, matches its description, and is legitimate. No inspection, no listing. Period.

4
Approval

Listing goes live with a verified badge

Once approved, the listing is published with an inspection report attached and a Domivault verified badge — so every buyer knows it's real.

5
Transaction

Buyer connects and closes the deal

Buyers search verified listings, message agents directly inside the platform, and close deals with complete confidence.
